## Style Prompt

Serious automotive risk warning for an official lubricant account. The visual language is restrained, technical, and credible: a clean talking-head frame with a white wall, black shirt, measured camera movement, and urgent captions that feel like a professional warning rather than a sales ad.

## Colors

- `#0B0F14` background black for warning bands and end card
- `#F7F8F5` primary text on dark surfaces
- `#D92323` danger accent for risk words and underline pulses
- `#F0B429` caution accent for price/source callouts
- `#6E7681` muted secondary labels

## Typography

- Chinese-first sans stack: `Microsoft YaHei UI`, `PingFang SC`, `Arial`, sans-serif
- Numeric/source labels: `Consolas`, `Microsoft YaHei UI`, monospace

## What NOT to Do

- No decorative gradients, bubbles, or busy motion.
- No exaggerated influencer-style captions.
- No cheerful advertising colors.
- No fake brand marks or unverified certifications.
